Our Mission

To give out sports equipment to the underprivileged youth globally.

What do we do?


We welcome donations from the public and private sectors to purchase sports equipment for the underprivileged students around the world.




How can you help?


You can use the contact page on the bottom of this page to get in touch with us to donate any amount of money. This money will used to buy sports equipment for the underprivileged.




What do we give out?


We give out a variety of sports equipment, from sports such as basketball, football, soccer, and tennis.
