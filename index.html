Our Mission

Empowering physical activity globally through varied fundraising and donations of sports equipment.

What do we do?


Our organization raises funds through hosting sports tournaments and fundraising for donations from the public and private sectors. Using funds collected, we purchase sports equipment for underprivileged students around the world. We collect almost-new sports equipment for schools and clubs in need of them, and we string torn tennis and badminton rackets to give them a new life.




What do we donate?


We give out a variety of sports equipment, including basketballs, soccer balls, soccer nets, jumpropes, tennis rackets, and tennis balls.




How can you help?


You can use the contact page above to get in touch with us to donate any amount of money (donations are tax-deductible). This money will used to purchase sports equipment for the underprivileged.
